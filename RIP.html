Routing Information Protocol (v2)

How to make a RIP route on a router In this example, lets just say you want to make it so people (from an ISP cloud) can access your server (that is located at 18.0.0.2) on your router, with RIP routing you can do just that!

Router>enable
Router#conf term
Router(config)#router rip

It is highly recommended to use RIPv2, which is newer. We will do that

Router(config-router)#version 2
Router(config-router)#network 18.0.0.0

Now outside computers should now be able to connect to the server
